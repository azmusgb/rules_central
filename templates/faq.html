What is Rules Central?

Rules Central is a platform for managing, visualizing, and collaborating on business rules.

What file types are supported?

Rules Central accepts .json and .mmd files for upload.

How do I view the hierarchy?

Open a diagram and click the Hierarchy button to explore rule relationships.

Can I export diagrams?

Yes. Use the Export option in the viewer to download images or raw files.

How do I share diagrams?

Open a diagram and copy its URL or use the Share button to send it to your team.

Is there an API?

Yes. Refer to the API section of the documentation for available endpoints.

How do I get started?

Sign up, upload your first diagram, and explore the dashboard for insights.

How do I upload a diagram?

Go to the Upload page and drag & drop your files or select them from your computer.

Can I customize diagram themes?

Yes! Visit the Settings page to create or modify themes.

Where can I get support?

Check the documentation or email support@rulescentral.com.
