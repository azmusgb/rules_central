What is Rules Central?

Rules Central is a platform for managing, analyzing, and visualizing business rules through diagrams.

How do I upload a diagram?

Navigate to the Upload page and drag & drop your JSON files or select them from your computer.

How do I extract rules from FormWorks documents?

Use the Rules Extraction Utility to process FormWorks documents and generate diagrams.

Can I customize diagram themes?

Yes. Visit the Theme Manager to create or modify themes for your diagrams.

Where can I get additional support?

Check the full documentation or email support@rulescentral.com.
