Yosemite National Park

Yosemite is a photographer's paradise! From towering granite cliffs to serene waterfalls, it's a place that never ceases to amaze.

Enter the park before 8:00 AM

Parking lots fill up quickly, especially during peak season. Enter the park early to avoid traffic and enjoy scenic spots with fewer crowds.
